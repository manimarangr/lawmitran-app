Refund & Cancellation Policy

For advocate subscriptions and document purchases. Last updated: 30 June 2026.

This is a draft template for the LawMitran product and is not legal advice. Adjust the windows and amounts to your commercials and have it reviewed before publishing.

This policy explains cancellations and refunds for paid services on LawMitran. Note that LawMitran charges advocates for subscriptions and users for documents; it does not collect advocates' professional fees — those are settled directly between the client and advocate and are outside this policy.

1. Advocate subscriptions

  • Free trial: new advocates get a 30-day trial; you are charged only if you choose a paid plan.
  • Cancellation: you may cancel anytime from your dashboard. Cancellation stops future renewals; your plan remains active until the end of the current paid period.
  • Refunds: subscription fees are generally non-refundable once a billing period begins, since the listing benefit is delivered continuously. If you were charged due to a clear billing error or duplicate payment, contact us within 7 days for a review and eligible refund.
  • Auto-renewal: plans renew automatically unless cancelled before the renewal date. Renewal reminders are sent in advance.

2. Document purchases

  • Before generation: if you have paid but the document has not yet been generated/delivered, you may cancel for a full refund.
  • After generation/delivery: because digital documents are generated to your inputs, they are generally non-refundable once delivered. If the delivered document is defective or does not match the template you purchased, contact us within 48 hours for a correction or refund.
  • Stamping / e-sign / physical delivery: third-party charges (e-stamp duty, courier) already incurred are non-refundable; only undelivered/unused components may be refunded.
  • Failed payments: if money is debited but the order fails, the amount is auto-reversed by the payment gateway, typically within 5–7 business days.

3. How to request a refund

Email support@lawmitran.com from your registered email with your order/payment ID and reason. We aim to respond within 3 business days. Approved refunds are returned to the original payment method, typically within 5–10 business days depending on your bank/gateway.

4. Taxes

Applicable GST is charged on platform fees and shown on your invoice. Refunds are processed net of any non-refundable third-party charges; tax adjustments follow applicable law.

5. Contact

For any billing question, email support@lawmitran.com.
